Lasagna

Description

Lasagna (plural “lasagne”) is a flat and expanded pasta sheet, traditionally made in Italy with Parmigiano-Reggiano (Parmesan cheese), Béchamel sauce (white sauce), and ragù (a meat-based sauce). Despite the traditional form still being popular, lasagna has taken on many different recipes over the years.

Ingredients

  • Lasanga: Approximently 12 shells.
  • Cheese filling: Use ricotta and parmesan with seasonings and a couple of tablespoons of parsley.
  • Meat: Use beef or chicken also pepperoni.
  • Sauce: Use regualr tomato sause or pasta alfredo for a little twist.

How To Make Lasagna

Homemade lasagna may have a few steps, but each step is easy – and I assure you it’s worth the time.

  1. Boil pasta: Boil the pasta for approximently 10-12 minutes.
  2. Prepare meat sauce:Cook Beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Combine cheese mixture:Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.
  4. Layer and bake:Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until browned and bubbly.
